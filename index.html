About Belshy Global Solutions

Our Business

Founded in 2019, Belshy Global Solutions is a professional services business based in Nairobi, Kenya. We provide comprehensive business solutions to individuals, organizations, and institutions seeking to enhance their performance and achieve their goals through Leadership Development, Educational Consulting, Training, and Event Planning.

Operating from Maendeleo House in Nairobi, we specialize in delivering leadership development programs, educational consulting services, professional training solutions, and comprehensive event planning across various sectors and industries.
