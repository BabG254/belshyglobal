About Belshy Global Solutions

Our Mission

Founded in 2019, Belshy Global Solutions is dedicated to providing world-class training, mentorship, and coaching to young people across Kenya. We focus on empowering the next generation with the skills, knowledge, and confidence they need to succeed.

Operating from Maendeleo House in Nairobi, we've impacted thousands of lives through our comprehensive programs in character development, gender-based violence prevention, sexual reproductive health, mental wellness, and career advancement.
